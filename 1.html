Metacognition

MSDEP

Description

Metacognition is a section dedicated to a self-crafted process
that teaches students how to practice metacognition through active
data evaluation. It is turning passion and purpose into a plan. It is a
series of articles that explains why an iterative improvement cycle is
necessary for achieving goals, the steps in the process, areas and variables,
and how to start. I hope to create an AI that streamlines this process in
the future, as it already has a lot of similarities in terms of pattern
identification and data evaluation which I discuss in the Future Lens section.
