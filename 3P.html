A series of 3 essays—“Passion,” “Purpose,” and “Plan”—that define a “P”
in the 3P Mentality, a mindset whose application guides effective
learning, thinking, and planning

The “Ps” are the principles that define the mindset.
Passion: The intrinsic drive to continue doing what
one loves regardless of external validation
Purpose: A firm conviction guiding one’s fundamental
role in life
Plan: A specific framework whose execution fulfills
one’s purpose
