Passion. Purpose. Plan.

A digital showcase of essays, frameworks, and immersive
visuals that envision a passion-based approach to learning.
From the 3P Mentality to the Gallery of Thoughts
to a Future Lens, Project Passion uses creative works
to uphold a future educational framework that cultivates
initiative and creativity. The Vision, PP’s home page,
features a Site Map and foundational essay “The Hustle”
to discuss our vision in detail.

Site Map

"TH"

“The Hustle” is Project Passion’s foundational
essay whose rejection of Hustle and advocacy of
passion forms the website’s philosophy, where
learning, thinking, and planning become intrinsic
processes. In its five sections, from “The Hustle”
to “Our Plan” we argue for a passion-based approach
to learning, discuss website’s structure and purpose,
and a proposal for a "Maker Course."
